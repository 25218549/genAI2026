# Program Overview

## Background
This program is organized by Dr. Simon Wang, who has been instrumental in developing a curriculum that bridges the gap between traditional education and modern technological advancements.

## Difference of Copilot Agent from Ordinary AI
Unlike ordinary AI systems, the Copilot Agent is designed to assist users by providing contextual and relevant support, enhancing productivity and learning efficiency in real-time.

## Invitation to Apply
We invite students from both programming and non-programming backgrounds to apply, as the program is inclusive and aims to promote diverse learning experiences.

## Added Benefits
Participants will have the opportunity to engage in:
- Service learning course participation, which fosters community engagement and practical application of skills.
- STEM internships that offer real-world experience and exposure to industry practices.
- Research opportunities for students pursuing PT (Part-Time) or PhD studies, allowing for advancement in their academic careers.

## Credly Certification
Participants will receive Credly certification upon completion, affirming their skills and achievements in the program. This certification can enhance their resumes and career prospects.
